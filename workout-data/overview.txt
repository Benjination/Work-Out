Ben's 30-Minute Workout Overview

Total Duration: 30 minutes

Structure:
• 5 minutes: Warmup
• 15 minutes: Strength circuit (no sets, no repeats)
• 8 minutes: Easy cardio (bike or gazelle)
• 2 minutes: Tai chi/yoga cool-down

General Guidelines:
• Aim for about 40 seconds work, 20 seconds to transition between moves
• Use very light weight or bodyweight
• Move continuously with low impact
• Listen to your body and modify as needed
