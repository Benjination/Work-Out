Strength Circuit (5:00-20:00)
Duration: 15 minutes total
Goal: One pass through circuit, ~40 seconds work, ~20 seconds transition

Step 1 (5:00-6:00): Hip hinge / Romanian deadlift
• Use bar or dumbbells
• Soft knees, hinge at hips
• Feel stretch in hamstrings
• Squeeze glutes to stand
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 2 (6:00-7:00): Goblet or bodyweight squat
• Hold a plate or dumbbell at chest if comfortable
• Or just bodyweight
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 3 (7:00-8:00): Bent-over row
• Use bar or dumbbells
• Hinge slightly
• Pull weight toward lower ribs
• Squeeze shoulder blades
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 4 (8:00-9:00): Incline or wall push-ups
• Hands on counter/wall/bench
• Straight body
• Controlled tempo
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 5 (9:00-10:00): Standing overhead press
• Very light weight
• Press slowly up
• Don't lock out hard
• Stop if shoulders complain
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 6 (10:00-11:00): Static lunge (right leg forward)
• Small range
• Slight bend
• Hold onto something if needed
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 7 (11:00-12:00): Static lunge (left leg forward)
• Small range
• Slight bend
• Hold onto something if needed
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 8 (12:00-13:00): Standing calf raises
• Hold support
• Rise onto toes
• Pause at top
• Slow down
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 9 (13:00-14:00): Dead bug (on back)
• On back, knees bent 90°
• Press low back into floor
• Alternate tapping heels or extending one leg at a time
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 10 (14:00-15:00): Side plank (right, modified if needed)
• On forearm and knees
• Keep straight line from knees to head
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 11 (15:00-16:00): Side plank (left)
• On forearm and knees
• Keep straight line from knees to head
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 12 (16:00-17:00): Glute bridge
• On back, feet on floor
• Squeeze glutes to lift hips
• Pause, lower slowly
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 13 (17:00-18:00): Standing band/plate front raise (optional)
• Light plate or band
• Raise arms to shoulder height
• Control down
• Duration: 1 minute

Step 14-15 (18:00-20:00): Easy "flow"
• Repeat your favorite 2-3 movements lightly
• Examples: bodyweight squats, bent-over no-weight rows, glute bridges
• Very low effort just to keep moving
• Duration: 2 minutes
