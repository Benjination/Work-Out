Warmup (0:00-5:00)
Duration: 5 minutes
Goal: Move continuously, low impact

Step 1 (0:00-2:00): Easy march + arm circles
• March in place
• Swing arms gently
• Add forward/backward shoulder circles
• Duration: 2 minutes

Step 2 (2:00-3:30): Torso and hip mobility  
• Gentle torso twists
• Side bends
• Slow hip circles
• Duration: 1.5 minutes

Step 3 (3:30-5:00): Light squats and knee lifts
• Shallow bodyweight squats
• Alternating knee lifts with a little core brace
• Duration: 1.5 minutes
